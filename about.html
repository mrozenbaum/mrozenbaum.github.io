I am an adventure seeker, and a go getter!
I design and build Websites, Applications, and Digital Products.
Growing up with multiple cultural backgrounds, and living in different countries has taught me how to view even the simplest every day things differently, to think out side of the box, and to understand peoples needs.
My experience, knowledge, and background with multiple cultures and people, has given an advantage when I am designing and developing for a specific user.
I take my projects and research very seriously, and I enjoy working with creative and like minded people.

I have a huge love for Data, Design, Research, and Data Architecture.
I am looking to grow and develop my craft as a software developer and a designer. I would love to join a team that will support, challenge, and guide me to be the best at what i do.
Designing and Developing gives me the ability to be creative, learn new things everyday, and to make the world a better place.
